About Us

VIKKI EnviroHealth Solutions is a leading provider of environmental and health regulatory compliance solutions. We specialize in obtaining TNPCB approvals such as CTE (Consent to Establishment) and CTO (Consent to Operate), as well as biomedical waste authorization. We also assist hospitals, clinics, and industrial projects in registration under the Clinical Establishment Act, approved by the Joint Director of TN Health Directorate of Medical and Rural Health Services. Our mission is to ensure regulatory compliance and environmental sustainability for our clients, offering expert consultation and streamlined approval processes.

Our Services

CTE - Consent to Establishment

We help you obtain the necessary approvals to establish your business under TNPCB regulations.

CTO - Consent to Operate

Our team ensures you get the consent to operate your business in compliance with TNPCB standards.

Biomedical Waste Authorization

We assist healthcare facilities in getting authorization for the safe management of biomedical waste.

Hospital and Clinics Registration

Register your hospital or clinic under the Clinical Establishment Act with our expert guidance.
